Employment Support

From discovering a direction to preparing for work

Employment is an important path toward empowerment. Through employment coaching, connections with entrepreneurs, and technical and vocational education, the institute helps women identify paths suited to their abilities and circumstances.

01

Employment Coaching

Support in recognizing strengths, clarifying employment goals, and preparing for practical next steps.

02

Skills Education

Using technical and vocational partnerships to strengthen skills that can be applied in the workplace.

03

Entrepreneur Connections

Building relationships with entrepreneurs, particularly in fields related to women’s work, to identify realistic opportunities.

04

Continued Support

Coordinating benefactors and partner organizations around needs that may otherwise interrupt education or access to employment.

Lasting empowerment

Employment support is more than introducing an opportunity

Long-term outcomes depend on education, personal readiness, connections to the workplace, and complementary support working together.
