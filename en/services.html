Services

Support built through collaboration and an understanding of real needs

Our services focus on supporting women affected by social harm. Some are delivered directly by the institute, while others become available through the capacity of partner institutions, professionals, benefactors, and entrepreneurs.

01

Social Support Coordination

Understanding individual circumstances and connecting women with institutions able to provide suitable support.

02

Education and Workshops

Delivering educational workshops and using courses and learning opportunities offered by partner organizations.

03

Benefactor Connections

Connecting benefactors’ capacity with needs related to social support and women’s empowerment.

04

Institutional Collaboration

Working with the Relief Foundation and organizations that recognize responses to social harm as part of their responsibility.

Services flow both ways

Learning and contributing within one shared network

Education and workshops may be delivered by the institute as part of its services, while the institute can also benefit from programs, specialists, and educational capacity offered by its partners.
