About Us

Connecting social, educational, and employment support

Based in Tehran, we support women affected by social harm. The institute does more than deliver isolated services: it builds purposeful, lasting connections between women’s real needs and the people and organizations with the capacity to help.

Collaboration is central to our work

Agreements and relationships with the Imam Khomeini Relief Foundation, entrepreneurs, benefactors, and technical and vocational organizations help ensure support is not limited to an immediate need. Together, they open access to education, skills, employment, and continued social support.

We also draw on institutions that consider responding to social harm part of their mission or social responsibility. This network strengthens the institute’s ability to offer broader support shaped around each woman’s circumstances.
